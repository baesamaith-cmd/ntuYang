Overview

Research that connects theory, systems, and production

The Design Sciences Laboratory develops methods and technologies for customized and sustainable engineering systems. Our work spans design optimization, additive manufacturing, embedded sensing, digital twins, and software-defined production environments.

Assoc. Prof. Moon Seung Ki Principal Investigator, MAE NTU Singapore
Current theme Smart manufacturing, digital twins, and sustainable system design

Research

Core research directions

Structured around manufacturing intelligence, product architecture, and sustainable engineering systems.

Design Optimization

Strategic and multidisciplinary design optimization for complex engineering systems and configurable production platforms.

Advanced Manufacturing

Additive manufacturing, process modeling, and production innovation for next-generation manufacturing environments.

Digital Twins

Software-defined products and production twins for prediction, reconfiguration, and operational intelligence.

Sustainable Design

Methods for modularity, remanufacturing, lifecycle decisions, and long-term sustainable system planning.
