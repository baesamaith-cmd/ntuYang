Designing sustainable systems for the factories and products of tomorrow.

We bring together design science, economic theory, and AI to rethink manufacturing systems, digital twins, product-service architectures, and sustainable engineering decisions.

Our approach

Research that connects design, intelligence, and production.

The lab develops methods and technologies for customized and sustainable engineering systems, with work spanning design optimization, additive manufacturing, embedded sensing, and software-defined production environments.

Research

Core directions

Structured around manufacturing intelligence, advanced engineering design, and sustainable system thinking.

Design Optimization

Strategic and multidisciplinary design optimization for engineering systems, decision structures, and configurable production platforms.

Advanced Manufacturing

Additive manufacturing, process modelling, and production innovation for next-generation manufacturing environments.

Digital Twins

Software-defined products and production twins for prediction, reconfiguration, and operational intelligence.

Sustainable Design

Methods for modularity, remanufacturing, lifecycle decisions, and long-term sustainable engineering systems.
